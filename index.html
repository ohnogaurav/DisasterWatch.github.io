About DisasterWatch

DisasterWatch is a platform that allows users to report, verify, and stay informed about natural disasters in real-time. We aim to create a community-driven, crowdsourced tool that enhances disaster response efficiency by leveraging real-time data from affected individuals.

How It Works

DisasterWatch works by allowing users to report disasters, which are then verified and displayed on a live map. By crowdsourcing data from those directly impacted, we provide accurate and timely information to help people stay safe and informed.

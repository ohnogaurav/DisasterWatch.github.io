Our Services

Real-time Disaster Reporting

DisasterWatch provides real-time disaster reporting features, allowing users to quickly submit details of natural disasters and emergency situations. This includes location, type of disaster, and severity level, helping to keep everyone informed.

Geolocation and Map Integration

Our platform integrates with geolocation services to automatically detect user locations and display nearby disaster reports on a real-time map. This ensures that users can see current conditions in their area at a glance.

Emergency Alerts & Notifications

Receive real-time alerts and notifications about natural disasters or emergencies happening near you. DisasterWatch ensures you stay informed and take the necessary precautions.

Multilingual Support

We provide support in multiple languages, ensuring that people from diverse backgrounds can access critical disaster information in their preferred language. This helps in effectively reaching a global audience.

Safety Resources & Guides

DisasterWatch offers a library of safety resources, guides, and checklists to help you prepare for, respond to, and recover from various disasters such as floods, earthquakes, or storms.

Data Aggregation & Analytics

We aggregate data from multiple sources, including government agencies and user reports, to provide accurate and up-to-date disaster information. Advanced analytics help authorities and first responders make informed decisions.
